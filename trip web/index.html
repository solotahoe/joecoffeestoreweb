The group of school of economics students together with the project planning students visited the western and Nyanza region of Kenya on the above mentioned dates. Our main aim was tour the western and Nyanza region studying the various economic activities taking place in those places and their economic importance to the people in the region.

We were in the factory for approximately two hours where we were being taken through the process of manufacturing tea by an expert in that field.
