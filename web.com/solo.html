In this case study I will be looking at how unemployment interferes with economic growth of a country. I will be comparing employment levels in developed countries like the USA, France, Germany etc with unemployment levels in developing and less developed countries like Kenya and Uganda respectively. I will also looking at economic growth on African countries and compare it to their respective levels of unemployment in order to establish the relationship between the two. I will further narrow down to how unemployment has been a stumbling block towards economic growth in Kenya. I shall look at the major causes of unemployment in Kenya as well as the remedies that the government can put in place to curb this menace. I further breakdown unemployment into different categories and do a deep analysis to each type to determine its causes and provide solutions for the same. I shall compare different economic growth rate of a country through a series of time in years and compare it to their respective unemployment levels at that time.
